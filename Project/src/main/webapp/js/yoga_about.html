Yoga

체내 노폐물을 효과적으로 빼내주고, 호흡과 명상으로 몸과 마음이 건강해지는 현대인의 맞춤 힐링 요가

The key of being mindful and living a balanced life begins with getting in touch with your body and understanding its close connection to your mind and overall state of being.

Vinyasa

몸을 역동적으로 움직이는 가운데 호흡과 동작이 하나되어 신체 핵심 근육 발달과 유연성에 도움이 되는 요가

Vinyasa is a form of yoga that has the power to restore balance and harmony in the body through dynamic flow sequences. Students will learn yogic style breath and the deep inhale and exhale movement flows improve practitioner’s oxygen levels in the blood and lung capacity. In addition, the regular practice of Vinyasa Yoga will help students to improve body awareness through the development of core muscles in the abdominals, back muscles, arms and legs.

Pilates

신체부위 별 반복 운동을 통해 몸의 근력을 길러주고 체형을 교정하는 현대운동

Pilates helps release the tension and strengthen your body. It is a practice that combines yoga and stretching suitable for those who need flexibility and muscle strength.
